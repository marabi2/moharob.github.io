TRACE Protocol

TRACE is more than a tool—it's a flexible product and supplemental overlay that works with your existing workflows. TRACE brings together automation, customizable dashboards, and AI-driven insights to give teams a real-time view of program health and priorities—without forcing you to change the way you already work.

How TRACE Works

Automation Bots
Automatically transcribe meetings, capture action items, and send reminders or collaborative emails if progress stalls.
Modular Dashboards
Drag and drop widgets for part health, resource demand, and critical metrics—see what matters most to you.
Newsletter Summaries
Subscribe to daily or weekly TRACE updates—a clear gameplan delivered to your inbox.
Custom Metrics
Use TRACE’s widgets to track program health, aging, or compliance—build your view with easy drag-and-drop.
